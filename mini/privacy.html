Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is SkillSwap's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website, and other sites we own and operate.

1. Information we collect

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent. We also let you know why we're collecting it and how it will be used.

2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect to operate our platform, facilitate skill exchanges, and communicate with you. Your profile information, including the skills you offer and your bio, will be visible to other authenticated users to allow the platform to function.

3. Data retention

We only retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide you with your requested service. What data we store, we'll protect within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or modification.

4. Sharing of Information

We don't share any personally identifying information publicly or with third-parties, except when required to by law.

If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact us.
