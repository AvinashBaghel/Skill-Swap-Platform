How SkillSwap Works

Three simple steps to start your skill-sharing journey.

01

Find & Connect

Browse thousands of skills shared by real people. Filter by category, rating, or location and send a connection request.

02

Plan & Exchange

Agree on a schedule, set learning goals, and decide what each person will teach. Flexible, fun, and fair.

03

Learn & Teach

Start your exchange sessions! Learn a new skill while sharing yours. Grow together and leave reviews for each other.
